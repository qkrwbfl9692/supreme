About our Pizzeria

A very modern pizza house, various types of pizzas, pasta, dise dishes and more. Less salt, more fresh toppings with an affordable price.
Our pizza dough is made with 7 grains, in a special unique recipie, so don't be surprised with the color or the seeds inside the dough. The toppings may differ depending on the season, trying to keep everything fresh and the best taste.
Our store was made to give pleasure to our customers by the taste, and our service. Walk-in is okay, but for to get the food more earlier, online or phone reservation will be the best!
